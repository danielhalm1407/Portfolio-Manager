## Score Narrative

When asked to score a market narrative:

1. Identify the core theme (e.g., electrification demand, gas supply tightness)
2. Extract directional signals (bullish/bearish) and the time horizon
3. Assess conviction level (high/medium/low) based on source quality and corroboration
4. Map to portfolio tilt: which asset classes or sectors are affected and in which direction
5. Output a structured theme brief with: source, narrative summary, scored tilt, confidence, time horizon
