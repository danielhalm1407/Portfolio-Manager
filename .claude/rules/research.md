When editing research files:
- Follow the format conventions in `research/CLAUDE.md`
- Each theme brief must include: source, narrative summary, scored tilt, confidence level, time horizon
- Move superseded research to `research/archive/` with a YYYY-MM-DD prefix — never delete
- Conviction write-ups belong in `research/conviction/`, not `research/themes/`
