# research/ — Claude Instructions

## Structure
- `themes/` — theme briefs: qualitative narrative scored into portfolio tilts
- `conviction/` — deeper fundamental write-ups per name or sector
- `archive/` — dated snapshots of past research (YYYY-MM-DD prefix)

## Conventions
- Each theme brief should include: source, narrative summary, scored tilt, confidence level
- Move superseded research to `archive/` rather than deleting
